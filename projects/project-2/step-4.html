4. PREPARING THE CHOCOLATE COATING

CHOCOLATE COATING

  • 100 gr. heavy cream (35% fat)
  • 100 gr. mascarpone
  • 100 gr. white chocolate ganache
  • 100 gr. chocolate diplomat cream
  • 40 gr. powdered sugar
  • Mix mascarpone and cold cream on low speed until combined.
  • Add icing sugar and beat on medium speed until stiff peaks form.
  • Fold in 100g of chocolate p√¢te de chocolate and 100g of white chocolate ganache with a spatula.
  • Refrigerate until ready to use.
