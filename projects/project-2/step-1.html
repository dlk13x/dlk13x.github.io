1. PREPARING THE CHOCOLATE SPONGE CAKE

Chocolate sponge cake

  • 180 gr. pastry flour or all purpose flour
  • 180 gr. egg whites (6)
  • 180 gr. white sugar
  • 100 gr. egg yolks (6)
  • 30 gr. cocoa powder
  • Beat egg whites at medium speed until foamy.
  • Gradually add sugar and increase speed until firm meringue forms.
  • Add egg yolks and beat until just combined.
  • Sift flour and cocoa powder twice over the mixture, and fold in with a spatula.
  • Pour into a 22 cm baking tin lined with baking paper.
  • Bake at 180Â°C for 45 minutes.
  • Cool on a wire rack and set aside.
