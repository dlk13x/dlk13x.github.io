3. PREPARING THE CHOCOLATE DIPLOMAT CREAM

CHOCOLATE DIPLOMAT CREAM

  • 170 gr. whole milk
  • 120 gr. mascarpone
  • 120 gr. heavy cream (35%)
  • 40 gr. egg yolks (2)
  • 40 gr. dark chocolate (70%)
  • 40 gr. white sugar
  • 10 gr. corn flour
  • 7 gr. cocoa powder
  • 3 gr. gelatin
  • Hydrate gelatin in cold water for 10 minutes.
  • Boil the milk.
  • Melt white chocolate in the microwave in short bursts.
  • Mix egg yolks, cornflour, sugar, and cocoa powder.
  • Gradually whisk in the hot milk.
  • Cook until thickened, then remove from heat.
  • Add gelatin and mascarpone, whisk, and refrigerate for 4 hours.
  • Whip cream to stiff peaks and fold into the mixture before assembling.
