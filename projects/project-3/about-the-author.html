DESTINI KITCHEN

Destini Kitchen, a devoted fan of Hikaru Utada, has spent years immersing herself in the music and life story of one of Japan's most influential artists.

With a background in graphic design and a passion for storytelling, Destini brings a unique perspective to Utada's journey.

Drawing from her extensive knowledge of Japanese pop culture and Utada's discography, she crafts an in-depth exploration of the artist's rise to international fame, her personal struggles, and her impact on the global music scene.
